FIELD NOTES

Contradictions, missing evidence, corrections and questions that are not ready to become conclusions.

OPEN QUESTION 001

What does “access” hide?

A service can exist on paper and remain unreachable in time, cost, language or social permission. Build a measurable access index.

CONTRADICTION 002

National progress, local failure

How should the book narrate improvement without allowing averages to erase districts left behind?

METHOD NOTE 003

Never confuse a map with a person

Geographic and group averages describe distributions. They do not predict individual worth or destiny.
