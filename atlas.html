DOSSIER WORKBENCH

One issue. Six ways to investigate it.

Use this before entering the full 422-investigation catalogue.

CLAIM

What exactly is being alleged?

Narrow the sentence until evidence could prove, weaken or contradict it.

FINDING

What is the strongest thing we know?

Separate scale, trend, association and causation.

CONTRADICTION

What evidence resists the story?

Progress, regional variation and counterexamples belong inside the dossier.

MECHANISM

How could the pattern be produced?

Trace institutions, incentives, resources, rules and decisions.

CONNECTION

Where does this issue travel next?

Link the dossier to Book chapters, datasets, maps and documentary records.

VERDICT

How certain should the reader be?

Proven, strongly supported, disputed, plausible, unsupported—or still open.
