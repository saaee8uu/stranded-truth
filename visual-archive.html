Images are evidence too

VISUAL ARCHIVE

Documentary photographs, archival pages, satellite imagery and official graphics. Every visual requires creator, date, place, source and rights status.

PHOTO RULE

No fake documentary imagery

AI-generated pictures will never be presented as photographs of real people, places or events.

CAPTION RULE

Who, where, when, source

A beautiful image without provenance does not enter the archive.

CONTEXT RULE

No poverty wallpaper

People are not decorative evidence. Images must explain a system, event, place or historical change.
