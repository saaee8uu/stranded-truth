CORE RESEARCH SHELF

Primary sources and historical archives.

NFHS-5 India Report

Health, nutrition, fertility, mortality and household indicators. Use for scale and group/state comparisons; do not infer individual destiny.

SRS Maternal Mortality Bulletins

Official maternal mortality estimates. Record reference period and confidence limits.

UDISE+ 2023–24

School, student, teacher and infrastructure records. Administrative data; definitions and reporting quality matter.

PLFS 2023–24

Employment and unemployment indicators. Always state age group, status concept and rural/urban scope.

Indian Education Commission, 1882

Historical primary document available through the Internet Archive. Use as history, never as evidence of present conditions.

Kothari Commission, 1964–66

Historical education commission report. Useful for tracing continuity and change in policy arguments.
